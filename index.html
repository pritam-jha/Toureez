FAQ

Common Questions

Is my payment safe on Toureez? ▼
Yes. All payments are processed through Razorpay, India's leading payment gateway, with PCI-DSS Level 1 certification. We never store your card details.
How do I know vendors are trustworthy? ▼
Every vendor on Toureez goes through a manual verification process by our team before their packages are listed. We verify business registration, GST, and contact details.
Can I pay in installments? ▼
Yes! Many packages support advance booking — you pay a percentage upfront and settle the balance closer to your travel date. The exact advance amount is shown on each package's booking page.
What's included in a package? ▼
Each package has a detailed inclusions and exclusions list. Common inclusions are accommodation, meals, transport, guides, and permits. Always check the package detail page before booking.
How do I contact my vendor? ▼
After booking, you can message your vendor directly through the Toureez app's built-in enquiry system. You'll also receive the vendor's contact details in your booking confirmation.
Is there a mobile app? ▼
Yes! Toureez is available on both iOS and Android. Download it for real-time notifications, offline itinerary access, and app-exclusive deals.

Our Story

Building India's Travel Marketplace

We connect passionate travellers with local experts who know their destinations best.

Mission

Make Great Travel Accessible to Everyone

India has some of the world's most incredible destinations, but discovering them authentically is hard. Too often, travellers end up with generic packages from large operators that miss the local magic.

Toureez was built to change that — by connecting travellers directly with local experts, small businesses, and passionate guides who know their region inside out.

From an Idea to India's Travel Marketplace

Toureez started with a simple frustration — finding authentic, fairly-priced travel packages in India was unnecessarily hard. Local operators had no digital presence, travellers couldn't compare options easily, and payments felt risky.

We built Toureez to solve all three problems at once: a platform where local travel businesses can grow digitally, travellers can book with confidence, and the whole experience feels seamless from search to review.
